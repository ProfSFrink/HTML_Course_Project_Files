Final Fight

Yoshiki Okamoto cites the arcade version of Double Dragon II: The Revenge as his basis for Final Fight

A side-scrolling beat 'em up released by Capcom in arcades in 1989. Set in the fictional Metro City you play as a group of vigilantes and go up against the Mad Gear Gang who have kidnapped the mayor's daughter. You can play as one of three characters Cody, Guy and Mike Haggar the mayor of metro city, each of which play differently. Final Fight is the game Sega used as a bases for there own Streets of Rage franchise on the Sega Mega Drive. COMPLETED ON 13/05/2022

The King of Dragons

The King of Dragons is a side-scrolling beat-'em-up released by Capcom that has the player playing as one of serveral characters in a quest to defeat the dragon Glidiss that is terrorizing the kingdom of Malus. It has some lite RPG elements as your character levels-up as they play through the game and collect better equipment. The player can play as one of several classes which are inspired by the table top board game Dungeons and Dragons. COMPLETED ON 14/05/2022
