Super Famicom / Nintendo

The Super Nintendo (Famicom in it's native Japan) was a 16-bit console released in 1990 in the United States and is a successor to the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES).

Sega Genesis / Mega Drive

The Sega Megadrive (known as the Genesis in the United States) was 16-bit console released in Japan in 1988 and is the succesor to the Sega Master System
